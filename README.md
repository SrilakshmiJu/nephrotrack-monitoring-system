# nephrotrack-monitoring-system
A web based monitoring system to help families and health care centres to efficiently manage and track nephrotic syndrome treatment records. The system help users maintain organized records of urine protein levels, medication schedules, steroid dosage adjustments, relapse history, remission periods and hospital appointments for patients.

Patients with nephrotic syndrome often require continuous monitoring of urine protein levels and frequent medication adjustments depending on protein readings. maintaining these records manually using note books or scattered notes can become difficult especially during relapses or when monitoring multiple patients.

The system is designed to support families and care centres by providing a simple and organized platform to manage patient treatment information , users can record daily urine protein readings, maintain medication schedules, monitor steroid tapering plans , track relapse and remission periods and mange doctor appointments.

The system will provide reminder notifications for medication schedules, upcoming appointments and daily protein monitoring.

Dashboard summaries will allow users to quickly view current treatment status, last protein readings, active medication schedules and upcoming appointments.
